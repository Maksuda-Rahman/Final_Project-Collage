4 years “Diploma-in-Engineering Course” :

The main goal of this institution is to implement the international standard and contemporary technical education program formulated by the Government of the People's Republic of Bangladesh with the aim of building skilled manpower in technical education for the overall development of the country. To implement this goal, Rajshahi Women's Polytechnic Institute is currently conducting a 4-year Diploma-in-Engineering education program in 5 technologies. The technologies are:

  • Computer
  • Architecture
  • Electromedical
  • Electrical
  • Food
