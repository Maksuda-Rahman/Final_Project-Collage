History of Rajshahi Mohila Polytechnic Institute :

Rajshahi Mohila Polytechnic Institute is the only government women polytechnic institute in North Bengal. [1] Rajshahi Mahila Polytechnic Institute is a government educational institution located on the Bypass Road of Rajshahi Naodapara. Rajshahi Mahila Polytechnic Institute was established in 2003 and started its education program from the academic year 2005-2006 with a total of 152 students in 4 departments
The institute has a 4-storey academic building and a 3-storey workshop building. There is an acti library with about 1500 books. There is a four-storey hostel which can accommodate up to 300 students. Academic and administrative work is conducted by 60 teachers and 45 staff.
On the east side of the main building of the institute there is a 4-storey student residence with 148 seats. Seats are allocated among the students considering their merit and the distance of the institute from their permanent address. see more .
